    About Me

    Ever since he was 14, Cody has had a burning passion for entrepreneurship, graphic design, and branding. He got his first chance to prove himself at the age of 15, where he entered a design contest on 99 Designs for an apparel company called: STNLY SUPPLY CO. Shortly after winning the contest, STNLY hired Cody on to develop all their branding and apparel design. By the age of 16, Cody had spread his wings and flew. He had co-started up a successful clothing company called Indigo-T-Shirts, had taken on numerous design jobs for startups, had illustrated a storyboard for a motion-picture and had created his own brand Retrolection whom he is the CEO of. Through Retrolection, Cody's opportunities went through the roof and by the age of 17 he was working with local AZ startups such as, MASKD, and FEEL FREE. Through these connections Cody built a reputation for his knowledge for business, eye for design, and passion for branding. And through that he was invited to be a panelist at his chapter of HOUSE OF GENIUS not knowing that he would become youngest person ever to be on the panel for HOUSE OF GENIUS at the age of 17.
