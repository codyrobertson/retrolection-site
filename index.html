Services

Well, to start things off; I create living identities for my clients, I help them with the 3 D's of branding: Discover, Define, Design. If you want to find out more about this check this out but that’s where the magic happens. I believe in the importance of learning as much as you can and developing a set of ‘soft-skills’ that you can use to help you with areas outside of your trade. Thus, I am well equipped for most situations.

Identity Design

I create visual identities which are the overall look of a brand's communications.I achieve this by creating a consistent look and feel utilizing particular visual elements to create distinction, such as specific fonts, colors, and graphic elements.

Graphic, Web, UI, Design

Throughout my years designing I have grown accustomed to various skills in the realm, Graphic Design, UI/UX Design, and of course Web Design are parts of my passion for DESIGN in general. I also incorporate my abilities in these areas when i do Identity Design.

Branding

A brand is more than a logo. It’s a comprehensive experience that communicates to your audience who your company is. Beginning with visual identity, it also includes web interfaces, imagery, and communications. There is no limit to the involvement a consumer will have with a brand. And I can help you with all of it.

Brand Discovery

The discovery process will help me gain an in-depth understanding of the breadth and depth of your project so that I can develop realistic project goals. These initial phases also give me insights regarding the best path for your brand, and how to tailor my approach to ensure success in our project and in every aspect of the project.

Brand Definition

Here I dive deeper into researching your existing or future markets, competitors, audiences, personas, persona motivators, and even specific points of interaction. This process gives me a vibrant and clear picture of where we need additional research on your company and industry in order to present you with informed recommendations for the next phase of the project: Design.

Brand Desinging

This Stage: I design everythign you need to put your best face forward, the design process encompasses visual identity; covering basic branding—logotype, mark exploration, secondary typography, color palette—to additional elements like app icons, iconography, illustration, patterns, and more..
